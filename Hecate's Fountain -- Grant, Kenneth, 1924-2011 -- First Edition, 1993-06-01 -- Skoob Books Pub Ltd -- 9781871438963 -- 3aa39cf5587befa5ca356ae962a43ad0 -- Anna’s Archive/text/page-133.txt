It may be noted, in this context, that there is a detailed account in the Necronomicon of a banishment, and incarceration within the earth, of a race of coleopterous entities. There are also hints of it in AL, and in the sinister Grimoire known as Liber Carcerorum vel Oliphotb,47 which Crowley published in The Equinox. The seals upon the cells of this buried and tunnelled city were broken open by Crowley,48 and it was a denizen of this realm that transmitted to Nerik the fragments that finally drove her to madness.
