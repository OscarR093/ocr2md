The exhortation to understand may indicate that these operations can be fully performed only in the power- zone of Saturn (Set), which lies adjacent to the Mauve Zone.

There then follows a beautiful dhyana or visualization of the mode of congress employed to facilitate this magical working. The priest is bathed in Nuit's starlight 6 which manifests as a "sweet- smelling perfume of sweat". This practically reproduces the descriptions applied by the Tantrics to the Suvasinis or 'sweet- smelling ladies' of the Kaula Circle.7
