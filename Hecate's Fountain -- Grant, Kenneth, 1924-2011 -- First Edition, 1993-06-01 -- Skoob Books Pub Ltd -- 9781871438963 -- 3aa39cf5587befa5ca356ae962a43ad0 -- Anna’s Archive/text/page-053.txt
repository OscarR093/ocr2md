It is worth noting in this Lovecraftian context that Cameron lived as a child in the northernmost part of Wisconsin, which is a region associated with the Cthulhu Cycle of myths through August Derleth. The region contains a lake34 which covers a gate to the Mauve Zone, to which Snake Cultists directed by Michael Bertiaux resort at certain times of the year in order to invoke the Deep Ones.

Like Austin Spare, Cameron associated with a Witch- woman who had occult affiliations with ancient Indian cults that had retained unbroken a secret tradition of traffic with the Great Old Ones. The Narragansetts of the New England region, the Adena of Ohio, and the Lenape dog- rib Indians of California are known to have forged links with entities spawned in the Mauve Zone, and in the outer rings of Yuggoth.
