Moola plunged screaming into the waters, and what arose therefrom the senses were unable to register. It was no mere reflection, however distorted, of the metal tubing forming the model of the Tree, but a swaying mass of tentacles which wrapped themselves about Moola and dragged her into the depths. And although her body was spewed forth almost immediately in a rain of black fluid, the soul did not return with it. Moola remained mindless until her death in 1958.
